Our main areas

Outside

The outside area is a popular area, especially in the summer times. In this area you will be met by loads of weird scientific sculptures. We give you the opportunity to sit down and enjoy the many sculptures, moving around and taking closer looks.

Inside

The inside area is the main area were all the activities happen. This is were the main attractions are, its were we offer most of our scientific activities.

Movie Room

The movie room is an area were we show educating scientific videos. We have educational videos for both young, and a bit older audience. Watching videos about science is a great way to learn.
